# Privacy Policy for GLASS **Last updated:** December 25, 2025 GLASS (“the App”) is a utility application designed to help users write, view, and manage on-screen scripts and text overlays. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains what information is (and is not) collected when you use the App. --- ## Information We Collect ### Information You Provide * Text you manually enter into the app (such as scripts or notes) This information stays on your device unless cloud features are explicitly enabled in a future update. --- ### Automatically Collected Information At this time, GLASS does **not** collect: * Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number) * Location data * Contacts * Photos or media * Messages or communications * Advertising identifiers Basic, anonymous technical data (such as crash reports or performance diagnostics) may be collected by the Android operating system or Google Play for app stability and improvement purposes. --- ## How We Use Information Any information provided within the app is used solely to: * Deliver core app functionality * Improve performance and reliability We do **not** sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. --- ## Children’s Privacy GLASS is designed to be safe for users of all ages. * The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. * There are no social features, chat systems, or public content sharing. * No targeted advertising is used. If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided personal information unintentionally, they may contact us to request deletion. --- ## Data Storage and Security * User-entered content is stored locally on the device. * No data is transmitted to external servers unless future optional cloud features are enabled. * Reasonable safeguards are used to protect user data from unauthorized access. --- ## Third-Party Services GLASS does not integrate third-party services that collect personal data for advertising or tracking. Google Play services may collect anonymized data as part of standard app distribution and crash reporting. --- ## In-App Purchases At this time, GLASS does not include in-app purchases or subscriptions. If premium features are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly. --- ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. --- ## Contact If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at: **Email:** [synnerscyrcle@gmail.com](mailto:Synnerscyrcle@gmail.com)
