Privacy Policy for GLASS

Last updated: December 25, 2025

GLASS (“the App”) is a utility application designed to help users write, view, and manage on-screen scripts and text overlays. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains what information is (and is not) collected when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Text you manually enter into the app (such as scripts or notes)

This information remains on your device unless optional cloud features are explicitly enabled in a future update.

Automatically Collected Information

At this time, GLASS does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media
  • Messages or communications
  • Advertising identifiers

Basic, anonymous technical data (such as crash reports or performance diagnostics) may be collected by the Android operating system or Google Play for stability and improvement purposes.

How We Use Information

Any information provided within the app is used solely to:

  • Deliver core app functionality
  • Improve performance and reliability

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

GLASS is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

  • The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • No social features, chat systems, or public content sharing exist.
  • No targeted advertising is used.

Data Storage and Security

User-entered content is stored locally on the device. No data is transmitted to external servers unless optional cloud features are enabled in a future release. Reasonable safeguards are used to protect user data.

Third-Party Services

GLASS does not integrate third-party services for advertising or tracking. Google Play services may collect anonymized data as part of standard app distribution and crash reporting.

In-App Purchases

At this time, GLASS does not include in-app purchases or subscriptions. If premium features are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
